How it works

Three steps to ship.

Open your project

Install JMart and point it at a project folder. Your codebase indexes locally on first open.

Describe a goal

Fix a bug, draft a feature, refactor a module, design an API. The agent reads, plans, runs.

Watch and verify

Every action appears in the activity log. Approve, edit, or re-run anything you need to.

Privacy by design

Your work stays yours.

What stays on your machine

Your code, conversation history, agents, skills, and tasks. No exceptions.

What leaves your machine

Only requests you make to a cloud AI provider — direct from your computer to theirs. Plus a single version check on launch.

What we never do

Analytics. Telemetry. Required accounts. Selling anything to anyone.

Questions

Frequently asked.

Is JMart open source?

Not for now. The app is free to download during this pre-release period.

Will it be paid later?

Yes. One-time purchase that includes a year of updates. The version you bought keeps working forever; renewing extends update access.

Is it stable?

Pre-release — expect rough edges and changes between versions. Updates land on launch.

What AI can I use?

Almost any modern language model — top-tier cloud models or anything you can run locally on your machine.

Does it work offline?

Yes, for everything except calls to cloud AI providers. Local-only setups are fully offline.

What about Windows and Linux?

Coming soon. macOS Apple Silicon ships first.
